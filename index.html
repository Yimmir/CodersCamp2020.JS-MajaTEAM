how to play

The empire rises in strength. Their new massive space station called Death Star is a lethal weapon able to destroy whole planets. The entire galaxy is in danger and planets of the Rebellion are the first to be vanished. Your mission is to board the X-wing fighter and destroy the Death Star. Our intelligence discovered critical vulnerabilities in this it’s nuclear core and that’s our chance to cripple the empire. Unfortunately you will have to get very close to the station, only then you can precisely aim for the critical spot. Empire forces won’t be standing still during your mission so expect their resistance.

mission

Your mission is to destroy the Death Star. To do so you will have to correctly answer 20 questions by recognizing attached photo. But beware, the enemy TIE fighter is chasing you. Fail to complete your task in time and you will be defeated.

categories

Start the quiz by selecting the category in which you would like to test your Star Wars knowledge. The options are:

PEOPLE / VEHICLES / STARSHIPS

Then decide on the difficulty level. The higher the level the less time you have to complete your task.

timer

You can see your progress during the quiz. Your spaceship will be slowly moving toward the Death Star as you correctly answer the questions. Watch out for the enemy fighter as you will lose if he get there first. Hurry up, the galaxy stake is in your hands.
